WaterBored Health App

WaterBored is a Java based Android Application that promotes healthy water consumption. The application takes into consideration one's gender, age, weight, physical activity, and other health considerations to determine how much water one should consume a day. It then breaks down the amount of water into healthy intervals, where the user is reminded to drink more water throughout the day. The user then can track their water consumption and see their progress over time.
